About Mulago Bright Standard Primary School

Mulago Bright Standard Primary School (MBS) is a Private Mixed Day and Boarding Nursery & Primary School located in UEB Zone, Mulago II Parish, Kawempe Division, Kampala.

Registered under the Ministry of Education, Sports and Information Technology with Registration Number PPS/M/150, MBS has been a pillar of hope for 27 years, providing quality, affordable, and compassionate education to vulnerable children in the Mulago community.

We serve children from the most challenging backgrounds — orphans, children of single mothers, and families living in extreme poverty.
